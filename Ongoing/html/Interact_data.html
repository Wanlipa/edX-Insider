Conclusion: Let people interact with your data

This is a technical conference. You and your students can code. Think about how effective you can be if you let people interact with your data, whether by looking at graphs before telling a story about them, through a simple JavaScript interactive as on the previous page, by embedding a Python program researchers can manipulate and modify, or by allowing people to download that data onto their own system (if possible within the time bounds of the presentation, or as an auxiliary resource).
