Students discovered -- for themselves -- the apparent paradox that a better hospital might have a higher mortality rate than a worse hospital. In the next video, Marcello tied together what the students discovered, and began to walk them through a derivation of standardization and normalization. 

One key point is that the students did the algebra. There are few things as disengaging as watching someone else do math. By being walked through the math, but doing the derivation themselves, students both learned more and were more engaged with the course.
