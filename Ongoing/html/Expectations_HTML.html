Experiential Learning -- do not skim

This course is a form of experiential learning. It will take about an hour to complete. To learn from this course, you should not only pay attention to the content of this course module but also to the instructional design.

People who skimmed this course universally found they got very little out of the process. People who went through and finished the course generally found the process rewarding. Please block off enough time to watch all videos and attempt all problems in this course.
