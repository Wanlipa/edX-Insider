Collaborative Dialogue Patterns in Naturalistic One-on-One Tutoring. Graesser, at al, 1995. 

This paper tries to find the reason for improved learning through tutoring by analyzing common patterns in tutoring interactions. A common pattern we can follow in design of learning sequences is: 

  1. Tutor asks questions
  2. Student answers questions
  3. Tutor gives short feedback on the quality of the student's answer
  4. Tutor and student collaboratively improve answer
  5. Assessment of student understanding
